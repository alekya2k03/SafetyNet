Abstract

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) Detection is a comprehensive industrial safety and surveillance system designed to enhance workplace safety and security. Leveraging live video streams from multiple areas within an industrial environment, it utilizes advanced computer vision and deep learning to ensure Worker Safety by detecting and promoting the correct usage of safety guards (helmet, safety vests, boots) using yolo v9. The purpose of PPE Detection is to enhance safety and security in industrial environments through advanced video surveillance and monitoring. PPE Detection relies on computer vision techniques, including object detection and recognition, to analyze live video streams from industrial cameras. Deep learning models, such as Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), play a pivotal role in identifying workers safety equipment. These models have the capability to accurately determine if workers are wearing helmet, safety vests, and boots.

Key Words: Computer vision, Object detection and recognition, Workers safety, Deep learning, YOLO v9.
